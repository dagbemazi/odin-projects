Margarita

Credit: Wikipedia

Description

Margarita is a cocktail onsisting of tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice often served with salt on the rim of the glass. The drink is served shaken with ice (on the rocks), blended with ice (frozen margarita), or without ice (straight up). Although it has become acceptable to serve a margarita in a wide variety of glass types, ranging from cocktail and wine glasses to pint glasses and even large schooners, the drink is traditionally served in the eponymous margarita glass, a stepped-diameter variant of a cocktail glass or champagne coupe.

Ingredients
  • 2 parts tequila
  • 1 part lime juice
  • 1 part triple sec or agave syrup
  • Fresh ice
  • Kosher salt or sea salt (optional)
  • Lime slice for garnish (optional)
  • A cocktail pitcher
Instructions
  1. Add ingredients, along with fresh ice to cocktail shaker.
  2. Moisten edge of glass with a lime wedge.
  3. Dip the rim on plate sprinkled iwth salt.
  4. Shake the cocktail "until frost forms on the outside".
  5. Strain it over fresh ice.
