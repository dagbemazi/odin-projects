Banana Strawberry Smoothie

Credit: selfproclaimedfoodie

Description

A smoothie or smoothy is a drink made by pur√©eing fruit and vegetables in a blender. A smoothie often has a liquid base such as fruit juice, dairy products, such as milk, yogurt, ice cream or cottage cheese.

Ingredients
  • 1 quart strawberries, hulled
  • 1 banana, broken into chunks
  • 2 peaches
  • 1 cup orange-peach-mango juice
  • 2 cups ice
Instructions
  1. Blend strawberries, banana and peaches together.
  2. Blend until fruit is pureed.
  3. Add juice and blend.
  4. Add ice and blend to desired consistency.
  5. Pour into glasses and serve.
